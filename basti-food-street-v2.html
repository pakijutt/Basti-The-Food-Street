Our Story

Where Islamabad
Comes to Eat

Basti The Food Street was born from one simple belief: that authentic Pakistani cooking deserves a home worthy of it. We opened on Golra Road with a small menu, a lot of passion, and a promise — every dish cooked the traditional way, every time.

A decade later, we serve hundreds of families daily across our grand dine-in hall and citywide delivery. The recipes haven't changed — and neither has our commitment to the richness of Pakistani cuisine.
